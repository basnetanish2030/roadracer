Instructions

  1. Press ENTER to start the game.
  2. Press SPACEBAR to fire the gun.
  3. Press UP ARROW ⬆️ to move the fighter car forward.
  4. Press DOWN ARROW ⬇️ to move the fighter car backward.
  5. Press LEFT ARROW ⬅️ to move the fighter car left.
  6. Press RIGHT ARROW ➡️ to move the fighter car right.
  7. You have 3 lives ❤️ for single run.
